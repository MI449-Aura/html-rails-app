If you or someone you know is struggling with an eating disorder, you are not alone and help is available. The National Alliance for Eating Disorders offers a helpline and therapist finder at www.allianceforeatingdisorders.com. You can also reach the Crisis Text Line by texting "NEDA" to 741741 for immediate support. Speaking with a doctor or mental health professional is always a great first step toward recovery.

If you or a friend are having scary thoughts about food or your body, it's really important to tell a trusted grown-up like a parent, teacher, or doctor. You don't have to figure it out by yourself because there are kind people who want to help you feel better! Remember, asking for help is one of the bravest things you can do.

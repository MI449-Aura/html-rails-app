About Us

The goal of our site is to make a website focused around safe and healthy eating habits. It contains easy recipes and foods to eat to give people healthy recommendations without needing to research themselves. It also holds information on eating disorders and ways to promote good eating habits.

About Us

Hi there! This website was made by students at Michigan State University. We wanted to talk more about healthy eating habits and eating a fun and balanced diet. We also wanted to talk about eating disorders and what they mean. They can be scary but remember asking for help is the bravest thing you or a friend can do. We all need food to survive and you are beautiful!
